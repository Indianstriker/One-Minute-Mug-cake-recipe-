One Minute Mug Cake
easy to make and delicious to eat

Chocolate mug cakes are great when you are running low on time and need a quick fix for your sweet tooth. So if today is one such day, it is time to grab your mug once again because we are here with the perfect recipe for you.










INGREDIENTS

4 tbsp all-purpose flour...
¼ tsp of baking powder
A pinch of salt
4 tbsp of milk
2 tbsp of sugar
2 tbsp of Cocoa powder
20 grams of Milk chocolate chunks or 2 tbsp of Chocolate syrup
2 tbsp of vegetable oil or Ghee
½ tsp of vanilla essence

PREPARATION

In a mug add the all-purpose flour,baking powder,salt and cocoa powder and mix well Then add the milk,sugar,vegetable oil or Ghee and vanilla essence, mix well Add the milk chocolate chunks or chocolate syrup and if want you can add sprinkles or chocolate chips, mix well Microwave the cake for 1 minute

Enjoy the delicious chocolate mug cake
